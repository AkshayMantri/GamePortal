# Catalog seed status

The production catalog currently contains two published proof records:

- **Chess** — scoped to traditional over-the-board play using the [FIDE Laws of Chess effective 2023](https://handbook.fide.com/chapter/E012023) as the factual and rules reference.
- **Go** — scoped to traditional physical play with the [British Go Association introduction](https://www.britgo.org/howtoplaygo), [rules overview](https://www.britgo.org/gorules), and [default-rules page](https://www.britgo.org/rules/approved) as the reference family. The record does not imply that one ruleset covers every regional or tournament variant.

Both sources were retrieved on 2026-07-17. The concise catalog summaries and the shared neutral placeholder SVG are original Game Portal material. Source rules, descriptions, and media were not copied or embedded; the catalog keeps reference-only links with field-level claims, attribution, verification evidence, and correction paths.

The physical Access Options describe self-provided materials only. `unrestricted` region state records the editorial scope of self-provided physical play, not a claim of universal legal or commercial availability. `en` records the language of the referenced instructions. No playable digital or store destination has been reviewed or published.
